External Vice President


Communicates with companies and organizations outside of UCI for collaboration

Requirements

1. All candidates must have at least a 2.0 cumulative GPA.


2. All candidates must be able and willing to serve the ICSSC for one full year and can only take a position without planning to stay for a year in the case that no other candidate offers to take the position.


3. Officers are not allowed to hold any position for more than 2 years.


4. By Fall 2019, the candidate must have been a member of ICS Student Council through a fall, a winter, and a spring quarter.


Note that as per Article VII, any requirement can by bypassed if the candidate wins by a majority vote with at least 15% of the ICS student body (425 votes) approving of the changes.

Please refer to the ICS Student Council Constitution for more details.
