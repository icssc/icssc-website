Apply to Our Committees

Students of ICS can apply to any of the following committees.

Public Relations

To inform ICS Students of the latest events happening in the school.

Projects

To develop projects for the council, other student associations, the Bren administration, UCI, and corporations.

Events

To plan and host events that will benefit ICS Students academically, professionally, and socially.

Corporate Outreach

To create and maintain connections with corporations and alumni to gather support for ICSSC events.

Alumni and Academic Affairs

To connect Alumni and Professors with ICS students to provide guidance in their academic and professional journey.
