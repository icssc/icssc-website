WebJam

ICS Student Council welcomes you back to school and back to another WebJam! WebJam is a week-long competition where students can form teams to design and develop fully functional web applications. This year's theme is "New Beginnings" to not only welcome students back on campus but also welcome beginners to learn something new and participate! The event is open to students of all technical levels and we will be providing workshops to help teach basic and intermediate concepts.

Missing Quarter: Bash Command Line Workshop

January 26th

Looking for safe ways to socialize before we go back in-person? ICSSC has got the BASH for you! Join us for the first installment of this year's edition of The Missing Quarter: a series of workshops about important topics in CS that are not covered in as much depth in classes! In this tutorial, you'll learn how to interact with your computer using the command line through an interpreter called Bash. While it's nice to have a graphical interface sometimes, the command line allows you to streamline certain actions, automate repetitive tasks, and will certainly make you a more efficient developer. Be sure to attend and add this extremely powerful tool to your toolbox!
